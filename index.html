What to do with the Image

If you are happy with the image you took then follow the following steps to create your own pick-up-line selfie!

First

Download the necessary .zip file by clicking on the icon:

Unzip the folder somewhere accessible such as your desktop

Second

Right click the webcam selfie you took above and save it to the /images/source folder

Rename the image as "white.jpg"

Third

Open up your terminal/command line and cd/dir into the unzipped folder

i.e. if my unzipped folder was on my desktop I would: cd /Users/[yourname]/Desktop/[unzipped folder]

Fourth

Depending on what pick-up selfie you intend to create look through the list of 'black' images. After selecting one resize your 'white' image to the same size and run the following code in your terminal/command line:

  • For frame - $python rotation.py
  • For spaceship - $python warp_affine.py
  • For ufo - $python warp_perspective.py
  • For sun - $python translation.py
  • For boxing ring - $python scale_shear.py

Your output will be appropriately placed in the output folder

Fifth

After transforming your image, take it from the output folder, rename it to white.jpg and readd it to the source folder. You can blend it with your pre-chosen 'black' image by running the following code:

  • $python gen_blending.py

Have fun with your newly generated pick-up selfie

Sixth

You can create as many pick-up-line selfies as your heart desires. Just repeat the previous steps
